对表58的解释：

1.表58-61均引自Kuhn E, Ayhan A. J Clin Pathol 2018;71:98–109.

2.表达模式：ARID1A核染色为野生型，不着色为突变型。β-cat突变为核阳性，野生型为胞质阳性。P53突变标准为胞核大于60%弥漫强阳性或者完全阴性。P53野生型为小于60%的阳性或者强弱不等核着色。

3.缩略词：

β-cat: β-catenin（β-连环蛋白）; calr: calretinin; CCC: clear cell carcinoma（透明细胞癌）; CK: cytokeratin（细胞角蛋白）; EMC: endometrioid carcinoma（子宫内膜癌）; GCT/SCST: granulosa cell tumour/sex-cord stromal tumour（颗粒细胞肿瘤/性索间质肿瘤）; HGSC: highgrade serous carcinoma（高级别浆液性癌）; LGSC: low-grade serous carcinoma（高级别浆液性癌）; M: mutant pattern（突变型）; MC: mucinous carcinoma（粘液性癌）; MMR: DNA mismatch repair genes（DNA错配修复基因）; mut:mutation（突变）; SCC-HT: small cell carcinoma of hypercalcaemic type（高血钙型小细胞癌）; SMT: seromucinous tumour（浆粘液肿瘤）; USC: uterine serous carcinoma（子宫浆液性癌）; wt, wild-type pattern（野生型）.
